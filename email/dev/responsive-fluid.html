What's more important than a Project Brief? A Project Debrief.

Just as a project brief gets a project off to a great start, a project debrief closes the loop and helps us in a variety of ways:
  • Encourages teams to evaluate processes and share key learnings in a timely manner
  • Adds value to our client work by tracking results and effectiveness
  • Proactively generates fresh content for our website, work samples, proposals, award entries, social media, etc.
Filling out a brief should only take about 30 minutes to an hour, so don't delay! For more information on the Project Debrief Process, see below. Happy De-Briefing!
